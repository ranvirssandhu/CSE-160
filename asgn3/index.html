ASGN3 Cow Farm World - Ranvir Sandhu - rsandhu6@ucsc.edu

Controls

W A S D move the camera.
Q and E rotate left and right.
Move the mouse on the canvas to rotate the camera.
F adds a block in front of you.
G deletes a block in front of you.
Click the canvas first so the mouse can control the camera.

Game

Lead the cow to the golden hay block.
