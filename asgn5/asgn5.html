ASGN5 Three.js Space Farm - Ranvir Sandhu - rsandhu6@ucsc.edu

Controls

Drag the mouse to orbit the camera.
Scroll to zoom in and out.
W A S D moves the ship.
Press R to reset the ship.

Scene Checklist

This scene uses more than 20 primary shapes, textured shapes, a textured OBJ model with MTL, a textured skybox, a perspective camera, mouse controls, animation, and multiple lights.

Wow Feature

Mini game: move the ship with W A S D and collect all the glowing fuel rings around the farm. Boundary walls keep the player inside the world.

Lighting

The warm light marker shows the point light. Use the slider to move it up and down.
